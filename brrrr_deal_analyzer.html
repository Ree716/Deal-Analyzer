How to embed this app in Notion
1
Download this file
You received this as brrrr_deal_analyzer.html. Save it to your computer if you haven't already.
2
Host it free on Netlify Drop
Go to netlify.com/drop, drag and drop your HTML file onto the page. Netlify gives you a free public URL in under 30 seconds — no account required.
netlify.com/drop
3
Copy your live URL
After uploading, Netlify shows a URL like https://random-name-123.netlify.app. Copy that URL — you'll paste it into Notion next.
4
Embed in Notion
In Notion, type /embed and hit enter. Paste your Netlify URL. Resize the embed block to fill the page. Your full interactive calculator will live directly inside Notion.
/embed → paste your Netlify URL → Enter
5
Optional: Custom domain
If you want a clean URL like deals.yourdomain.com, create a free Netlify account and connect your domain in the site settings. Takes 5 minutes.
6
Note on data persistence
Deal data lives in your browser session — refreshing clears it. For permanent deal storage, ask Throbe to add a local storage save/load feature, or export your deal summary from the Portfolio tab.
